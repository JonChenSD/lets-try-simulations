♦ arrows leave behind a sound when they disappear

  ricochet to redirect

♦ action blocks produce and continuously create for the world
  ⇦ ⇧ ⇨ ⇩ closed-arrow action blocks shoot an arrow in their
   designated direction
  ⇐ ⇑ ⇒ ⇓ open-arrow action blocks do the same at a slower rate

♦ animals carve and make way for the landscape serving as the orchestrators
  🐕 dogs will catch any arrows they come across, barking in joy
  🦌 deer will silently do the same
  🐂 oxen will push, move, or even destroy a block

♦ obstacles halt paths and can help shape the environment
  ⇈ fences are objects that animals cannot move through
